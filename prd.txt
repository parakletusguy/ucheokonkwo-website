

PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENT + Sitemap: Hon. Harris Uchenna 
Okonkwo Official Website 
 
1. Project Vision 
To create a "Constituency-First" digital platform for Hon. Harris Uchenna Okonkwo that 
breaks the barrier between the representative and the represented. By utilizing automated 
multilingual technology, the platform will ensure that every resident of Idemili North and 
South—regardless of literacy level or language preference—can engage with his legislative 
progress and campaign promises. 
2. Target Audience 
● Primary: Constituents of Idemili North & South (Igbo, English, Pidgin). 
● Secondary: Political stakeholders, ADC party leadership, and the Nigerian Diaspora. 
● Tertiary: Media houses and political analysts. 
3. Key Functional Requirements 
3.1 Multilingual Content Engine (The Core) 
● Input: Admin enters content in English. 
● Processing: Integrated linguistic engine generates versions in Igbo, Yoruba, Hausa, 
and Nigerian Pidgin. 
● Output: A frontend toggle that switches the entire content of the post without 
reloading the page. 
● Benefit: Allows Hon. Harris to reach the "unreached" in the markets and rural areas 
who prefer Pidgin or Igbo. 
3.2 Constituent Engagement Module 
● Project Tracker: A visual map or list showing projects completed/ongoing in Idemili 
North and South. 
● Digital Town Hall: A form for constituents to submit "Constituency Petitions" or 
feedback directly to the office. 
3.3 Campaign & Volunteer Management 
● Volunteer Portal: Simple onboarding for supporters to join the ADC campaign trail. 
● Resource Center: Downloadable campaign posters and manifestos. 
3.4 Admin Control Panel (The "Command Center") 
● Secure Login: Role-based access for the media team. 
● Review Workflow: Admin must be able to "Confirm" the Igbo or Pidgin translation 
before it goes live to ensure political correctness. 
 

4. Non-Functional Requirements 
● Mobile Optimization: Must be ultra-lightweight. Many constituents will access this 
via mid-range Android devices with limited data. 
● Security: High-level protection against "Defacement" or hacking (crucial for political 
figures). 
● Speed: Page load time < 2 seconds on 3G connections. 
5. Success Metrics 
● Accessibility: At least 30% of traffic coming from non-English language toggles. 
● Engagement: Number of volunteer signups via the portal. 
● Inclusion: Feedback from constituents feeling "heard" in their native tongue.
