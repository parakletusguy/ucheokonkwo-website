

Hon. Uchenna Harris Okonkwo: Brand 
Identity Strategy 
Following Hon. Uchenna Harris Okonkwo's move to the African Democratic Congress (ADC), 
his brand identity should strategically integrate the party's vibrant symbolism with his 
established professional background in luxury branding and legislative reform. 
The ADC Visual Identity 
The African Democratic Congress utilizes a high-vibrancy palette that distinguishes it from 
other major parties in Nigeria. 
● 
Official Colors: The party flag consists of equal horizontal stripes of Green, Yellow, and 
Red. 
● 
Symbolism: Green represents agricultural wealth and commitment to a land-based 
economy; Yellow signifies hope and prosperity; Red reflects passion and the struggle for a 
better Nigeria. 
● 
Key Emblem: A Handshake, symbolizing trust, mutual respect, and "warm acceptance" 
among all people. 
Recommended Brand Color Architecture 
To project a "Progressive Statesman" image that balances party loyalty with professional 
authority, the following tiered color system is recommended: 
● 
Primary Anchor: Integrity Navy (#002D62)
 
This should serve as the core color for official media releases and professional platforms 
like LinkedIn. It provides a sophisticated contrast to the bright ADC colors, reflecting his 
background as a luxury brand strategist and a "legal luminary". 
● 
Party Integration: ADC Yellow/Gold (#FFD700)
 
Transitioned from the previous "Prosperity Gold," this color aligns with the central stripe of 
the ADC flag. It should be used for headlines and highlights to signal energy, hope, and 
transparency. 
● 
Action Accents: Vanguard Red and Constituency Green 
○ 
Red (#D20D13): Use for urgent legislative updates or critiques to align with the ADC's 
Red stripe representing passion. 
○ 
Green (#008751): Use for documenting constituency projects in Idemili North/South, 
reflecting the party's agricultural focus. 
Strategic Implementation Across Footprints 
Importantly, these brand colors will be used consistently across the official website and 
all social media handles to ensure a unified and recognizable digital presence. 

● 
Unified Wordmark: Media releases should feature the ADC Handshake symbol integrated 
with a minimalist "Uche Okonkwo" wordmark to emphasize a "Promise of Trust" to his 
constituents. 
● 
Video Branding: On YouTube and TikTok, the use of the full ADC tricolor 
(Green-Yellow-Red) in thumbnails will maximize engagement with younger demographics. 
● 
Professional Presence: For LinkedIn and formal reports, the Integrity Navy should remain 
dominant to maintain a "premium" visual standard consistent with his published research 
on luxury systems. 
Conclusion 
By anchoring the vibrant ADC tricolor with a sophisticated Navy, Hon. Okonkwo can project a 
brand that is both politically aligned with the "Third Force" and personally authoritative.
